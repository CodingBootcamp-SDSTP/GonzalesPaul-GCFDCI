Gospel of Christ Fellowship Vision and Mission Statement

i. GCF Church Purpose

GCF Church exists to live out the Great Commandment and fulfil the Great Commission.

ii. GCF Vision

Love the Lord Jesus; be like Him and Disciple people to become like Him.

Articulated Vision:

As a Disciple-Making Cell Church, we are committed to.
- Love God passionately
- Grow I Christ-likeness
- Grow together as a loving family
- Become a disciple
-making people in Outreach & Missions

Vision Statement:

- We trust God to build GCF Church to be:
A Disciple-making Cell Church that produces Christ-like
worshippers and soul winners to accomplish the Mission God has for us.

iii. GCF Mission:

1. GCF, as a Disciple-making Cell Church, is committed to:
a. Transforming lives by Evangelism and Discipleship
b. Influencing the Community by advocating the family
c. Blessing the nations by establishing local churches
2. In short, “Transform Lives, Influence the Community, bless the nations”
